PROFILE

On 31st July 2018 I made the decision to leave behind a career in law and take the plunge into my passion, software development. It was on the aforementioned date that I began completing the prerequisites to gain a place onto the highly competitive Founders & Coders programme.

Prior to joining Founders & Coders, I was becoming more and more aware of technology and its impact on our everyday lives. I began following technology news and attending the occasional tech meetup. I have always been entrepreneurial and once I discovered the capabilities of technology, I began coming up with solutions to everyday problems all of which had technology at their core. Naturally, I did not have the skills or finance to build these ideas. A desire to not only create these ideas but understand how they were built lead me to learn how to code. As a result, I have discovered a real passion for software development.

I am now looking for a JavaScript Developer position, preferably with a tech stack that includes Node.js and React.js, which will allow me to build upon my one-year of software development experience.
