PROFILE

In July 2018 I made the decision to leave behind a career in law and take the plunge into my passion, software development. After this date, I began completing the prerequisites to gain a place onto the highly competitive Founders & Coders programme.

Prior to joining Founders & Coders, I was becoming more and more aware of technology and its impact on our everyday lives. I began following technology news and attending the occasional tech meetup. I have always been entrepreneurial and once I discovered the capabilities of technology, I began coming up with solutions to everyday problems, all of which had technology at their core. Naturally, I did not have the skills or finance to build these ideas, however a desire to not only create these ideas but understand how they were built led me to learn how to code. As a result, I have discovered a real passion for software development.

I am now looking for a Junior Developer position which will allow me to build upon my one-year of software development experience.
